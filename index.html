    MODULAR HOUSES

    Modular Houses are buildings constructed from pre-made modules (blocks) that are manufactured in a factory. Once produced, the modules are transported to the construction site and assembled into a complete structure. Advantages

    • Fast construction – buildings can be completed in a few weeks.
    • Energy efficiency – modern insulation materials improve thermal performance.
    • Flexible design – modules can be combined to create unique layouts.
    • Eco-friendliness – less waste compared to traditional construction.

    FRAME HOUSES

    Frame Houses are buildings constructed using a wooden or metal frame as the main structural support. The frame is then covered with insulation and finishing materials to create a durable and energy-efficient structure. Advantages

    • Fast construction – assembly takes only a few weeks.
    • Energy efficiency – good insulation reduces heating and cooling costs.
    • Lightweight structure – requires a simpler foundation.
    • Eco-friendliness – uses natural and sustainable materials.
