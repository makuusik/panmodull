MODULAR BUILDINGS

Modular construction in Poland is a technology where ready-made modules are produced in a factory and delivered to the construction site for quick assembly. This method is gaining popularity, enabling efficient construction of various structures. Currently, you can buy a modular home at an affordable price for different needs:

  • Recreational plots – a quick and economical solution for a summer house.
  • Residential homes – modern and comfortable, tailored to individual needs.
  • Offices – mobile and scalable workspaces.
  • Saunas – ready for use without long construction times.

FRAME HOUSES

Frame houses are gaining popularity due to their affordability, energy efficiency, and quick assembly. Modern technologies allow for the creation of comfortable and durable buildings that meet high standards.

Their key advantage is energy efficiency – good insulation minimizes heat loss, reducing heating costs. Additionally, a house can be built in just a few months.

Design flexibility allows for various concepts – from small houses to spacious residences. Eco-friendly materials contribute to a healthy indoor microclimate.
